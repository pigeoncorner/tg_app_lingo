Basic version:

"This paper talks about climate change. We looked at data and found that temperatures are going up. This is important because it causes problems. Other research says the same thing."

Advanced version with collocations:

"This paper addresses the pressing issue of climate change and examines the extent to which global temperatures are rising. Our analysis draws on recent research and provides compelling evidence that warming trends have significant implications for coastal communities. These findings corroborate previous studies and lend support to current climate models. The data clearly demonstrates that urgent action is required."
